The Troop

Three minds, one mission. Meet the people who make the magic happen every morning.

SO

Sarah O'Brien

Founder & Visionary

Former city-center restaurateur who traded concrete for chlorophyll. Sarah built Hungry Monkey on a simple belief: the best Irish food deserves a setting that lets you breathe. She curates every detail, from the Wicklow honey on your toast to the fern hanging above your table.

MH

Mark Hennigan

Head Chef

Trained at Ballymaloe, Mark brings a reverence for Irish produce and an obsession with bold, honest flavors. His kitchen mantra is simple: "Let the ingredients sing." He sources directly from Cork, Wicklow, and Dublin growers—no shortcuts, no compromises.

EK

Emer Kiely

Botanist & Curator

The green thumb behind our 100+ plant collection. Emer designs our living interior as a biophilic sanctuary, where every fern and philodendron has a purpose. She ensures the space doesn't just look alive—it feels alive, humid, and constantly growing.
